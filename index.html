My Education

Attending BYU–Idaho has been one of the best decisions I’ve ever made. The professors here genuinely care for students and go the extra mile to help us succeed, and the school culture is incredibly welcoming — friendly, faith-filled, and full of energy. I started in Winter 2024 and quickly found a passion for business analytics. I dove into classes that introduced me to tools like SQL, Python, Power BI, Excel, R, and even web design. I’ve loved learning how to connect data with real-world insights, and each class has stretched the way I think.
