Growing Up in California

I grew up in Sunny California with my parents and 6 other siblings. I love spending time with them at the beach, going on dirt toy trips in the desert, and riding rollercoasters at amuzement parks. I graduated highschool in 2021 and during this time I grew a passion for building computers, investing in the stock market, and playing lots of sports.

My Mission Experience

In 2021 of October I had the opportunity to serve a two-year mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in the beautiful state of Colorado. During my time there, I met many incredible people from all walks of life. It was a deeply meaningful experience that taught me the value of hard work, empathy, and faith. Serving a mission was one of the most formative and rewarding experiences of my life.

My College Experience

My college experience at BYU–Idaho has been both enriching and transformative. I discovered my passion for business analytics and I've learned a variety of programming languages and software tools. In addition to my coursework, I’ve had the opportunity to work on campus as a Career Success Mentor at the Career Center and as a Teaching Assistant for the SQL for Analysts class. Outside of academics and work, I enjoy country dancing, playing sports, and spending time with friends. These experiences have helped shape not only my skills but also who I am.
